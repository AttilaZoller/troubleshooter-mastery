Fast Progress
I am sure everyone wants to get new characters as fast as possible. Not recommeded if you are new to turn based tactics games. I highly advise you to use safety difficulty while doing story missions and hard/cruel when doing violent case.

Albus Responsibility is quite good.
Sion any.
Irene Lonely Hero must.
Anne Starlight Fragments.
Heixing Precision Fire.
Ray any.

1- First of all, go to options enable everything and set Quick Display to Super Fast, you will get used to speed in few hours. (Not Recommended if you want to take things slow and learn about game)

2- Play game on Safe Difficulty and choose Expertise company mastery, you will not lose anything in story missions. ( You will be grinding later on Hard, Cruel Difficulty later so just get used to game now )

3- Rest depends on you, I personally did all story missions until C2.7(Anne Recruit) without grinding items, masteries and lvl. Now you need to gear up a bit repeat 5 until you are lvl 15~17.

4- Go Mission Control and repeat first mission until you get Shukuchi Set. Skip this if you want to farm Shukuchi in 5.

5- Go Mission Control and repeat Backstreet of Magenta on Normal or Hard, open every box , kill every enemy until you are bored or feel ready.

6- Ideally you will have all masteries from 5th but if you don't thats alright. Although its not necessary you will want to have Retaliation+Shukuchi for Albus, Revenge+Shukuchi+Final Flame for Irene, Shukuchi is enough for Sion but if you want some damage Generator set and some cd masteries, Shukuchi for Anne, you will get healer set for her later for now just use her as 4th attacker.

7- After C3.3 Iron Forest Mountain repeatable will be unlocked. If you feel ready skip to 9.

8- if you need better weapons or Eluote for Albus repeat Iron Forest Mountain. Otherwise continue to 9. Eluote 15 lvl legendary 15% block, drops from Ralph at Hard+.

9- Now you need to beat hardest story mission in game C3.5 Tangled Skein. If you want to skip just reach all spots and save as many as possible.

A Guide for Troubleshooter
By: Botzu ᕕ( ᐛ )ᕗ
Well, the story map "Magenta Street" is fairly difficult on its own. Since clearing this one without losing any VHPD took me a significant number of tries as well as a bit of luck I figured I would write a little guide...


10- C3.6 Stop and Look Back is a bit tricky, since you can only use 3 characters. Just try your best.

11- C3.7 Adios quite easy. Make sure you choose to call White Tiger first. After this Heixing joins the team.

11- Now you will want to farm Slum of Shadow Fog for Anne healer set and legendary ring. Take a bit break from story missions and lvl your characters.

12- C4.1 Intruders all you need to do is gather 5 different psi-stones you don't have to kill monsters.

13- C4.2 Angry Tiger talk to bianca and alisa afterwards reach to phone as fast as possible and call albus, now just wait or go solo with Irene.

14- C4.3 Strength Competition send irene to left and rest of the team to right. Use VHPD if you need.

15- Iron Forest Resource Management District this mission is only for masteries and crafting. It is also good mission for leveling. Crafting became necessary for certain weapons for Ray, Heixing and Gisele.

16- It is very important to get unique masteries such as Material Collector and Mass Extraction. Please check the mastery drop section for details. It is must have to use Legendary Laborer when farming materials. But don't stary farming materials yet.

17- Your main aim is to get Gisele as fast as possible without wasting any time farming materials. Why? Because Legendary laborer gives 100% drop rate and x2 dropped materials. But Gisele hunter set gives 3x. Which means everytime she kills a beast she will get 6x materials with 100% chance.
Power Leveling For Humans & Beasts
There are two ways
1
Equip Insight on everyone and level everyone together. This is quite good if you want everyone to be at same level. Not recommended for end game.

2.1
Put Yearning on low levels and supporter on high levels this will give your low level characters double exp. Mostly used if your characers levels are higher then new character.

2.2
Put Learning, Understanding, Insight and Supporter on your highest level character and make only that character kill. This will allow low levels to earn more exp then your highest level character. This is esspecially useful if you like using only 1-3 character. Rest of the team can just stay and earn exp.

2.3
Use Challenge Mode and Play it on low level Missions. Make your low level characters kill enemies you will earn more exp when your level is lower.

2.4
Use Insight+Understanding+Learning+Supporter on Heixing and level any low level character in first 2 mission on challenge mode.
Since you don't need to bring albus to first 2 missions you can use any character you want.

-Use Gisele to take out patrolling enemy

Jurisdiction Guide
First you need to increase your reputation on every jurisdiction.

It no longer works.
1. Click Southern District Silver Cloud Jurisdiction.
2. Choose Civilian Cheer.
3. Lower difficulty to Safe.
4. Choose the very first mission "Backstreet of Silver Cloud". There are 12 enemies in Safe D.
5. I use Heixing to clear this mission. It takes around 1 minute.
6. First move Heixing to the middle. You will see 4 enemies, defeat them.
7. Afterwards check movement grid and see if there is blank "+" it means there are enemies there.


How to max enemy progress by using Jurisdiction.
1. Go to Wall District and choose Magenta Street.
2. It requires lvl 8 reputation, do the above process until you reach the needed lvl.
3. Choose Information Dealer.
4. Increase Difficulty to Normal or above. ( Note I haven't tried it on normal but Oliver should be available. If you cannot find Oliver switch to hard or above. )
5. Go to Mission Control and choose "Backstreets of Magenta" violent case.
6. Choose 1 Swat 5 VHPD and North.
7. Preferably you should be using Albus. It is best to have only 1 character here.
8. Use Turning over a new leaf skill and move albus to right side of the location as far as possible. Use Wind Walk afterwards.
9. You should be seeing Oliver now. If not Use Owl Eye or increase your Move Distance. I use md 13 here.
10. Retreat after you defeat Oliver. Send Albus back to start point. It takes around 1-3 min.
11. You get 25 information per mission which means 125 information after report. Bosses need only 25 information to max. Which means you can potentially max any boss after only meeting once. But do note that It is random and same enemy can get 2-3 information update.

This is the build I use for this mission. I also have 13 Move Distance.
Taming Guide
Requirements
  • Gisele
  • Tame Beast skill. Unlocked by default.
How to Tame
  • Tima/Yasha/Dorori/Negoori
  • Taming skill needs you to be unmoving for 10% of Beast HP as Action Time. It cannot be lowered or increased by any means.
    !!! DON'T USE KYLlE AWAKEN COMMAND !!!
  • During the process the beast must not get hit or taming fails.
  • You will notice taunted buff on the beast you want to tame take extra care to not hit accidentally or you will have to wait 6 turns to try tame again.( Can be lowered thanks to catharsis.)
  • Process is finished when its Gisele's turn again, regardless of whether she is stunned or has other debuffs taming succeeds.
Small tricks to use
  • In the taming process beast must not get hit but it doesn't mean you can't use restricting type debuffs such as Anne Constricting Root and Starlight Prison.
  • Starlight Prison prevents beast from moving and makes it immune to all damage. You can also use it on Gisele as well. In case you are surronded and don't want gisele to die.
  • Taming requires you to wait 10% of Beasts HP as action time. Which means the lower its HP faster you can tame it. Rainbow dorori is easiest beast to tame since it will go into hibernation and revive with 1 HP.

How it works
  • You can upgrade any item up to +9.
  • Upgrading item increases only base stat of item.
  • For example atk/esp for weapons,.
  • Upgrading item consumes X main material type according to item rank and Y general material.
  • X can only be obtained by salvaging same rank and type(weapon/armor) item and can only be used to upgrade same rank and type(weapon/armor).
  • Y can be obtained by salvaging any item type and any rank and can be used to upgrade any item according to its specific options.
  • Salvaging +1-9 gives 50% of materials spent.
